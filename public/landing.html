Features

Reminders that meet you where you are.

You pick the channel, the lead time, and the assignment statuses you care about. AutoRemind handles the rest, every day, automatically.

Multi-channel reminders

Get notified by email, SMS, or Discord DM, or all three. Pick the channels that actually reach you when it matters.

You're in control

Choose how many days ahead to be reminded, which assignment statuses to hear about, and which channels to use. Unsubscribe in a click.

Built for Berkeley courses

Connects to your course platform and pulls deadlines automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual entry, just reminders that match your real assignments.

A little extra structure, for everyone who needs it.
